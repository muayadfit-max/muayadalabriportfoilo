About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Chemical and Process Engineering graduate from Sultan Qaboos University (2025), with a strong foundation in process simulation, thermodynamics, heat transfer, and plant economics. My academic journey culminated in a senior capstone project on Blue Hydrogen Production from Flare Gas — simulated using Aspen HYSYS and assessed for techno-economic viability aligned with Oman Vision 2040.

My internships have given me real-world exposure: at Falcon Wireline & Drilling Systems I contributed to process safety documentation, SOPs, and HSE compliance within oil & gas field operations. At the Port of Duqm Company (PODC), I gained hands-on experience in industrial asset management, preventive maintenance planning, and equipment troubleshooting.

I'm deeply interested in CO₂ capture, distillation column design, heat integration, and sustainable engineering. I believe process engineering is key to decarbonising the energy sector — and I want to be part of that transition.

Outside engineering, I founded MuayadFit, a digital health and fitness brand in Oman, where I developed entrepreneurial, data-analytics, and partnership management skills.

Process Design PCC Production Plant Economics

Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC) Plant Proposal

A detailed plant design proposal for a Precipitated Calcium Carbonate production facility, covering raw material selection, reaction pathway (carbonation process), equipment specifications, and process flow diagrams. 🏗️ Distillation Column Design
Distillation Column Design Mass Transfer

Distillation Column – Detailed Engineering Calculations

A rigorous hand-calculation and simulation-supported study of distillation column design, covering feed tray location, reflux ratio optimisation, number of theoretical plates (McCabe-Thiele method), and column diameter sizing. The project demonstrates a thorough understanding of vapour-liquid equilibrium, tray efficiency, and separation performance — core competencies for refinery fractionation units such as CDU/VDU atmospheric columns and product splitters.

  • McCabe-Thiele & Fenske methods
  • Reflux ratio & tray efficiency
  • VLE & thermodynamic analysis
  • Column sizing & hydraulics
